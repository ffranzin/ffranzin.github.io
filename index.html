Flavio Franzin

· GAME DEVELOPER and TECHNICAL ARTIST · -remove-

A few months after starting my journey as a hobbyist 3D modeler, I landed my first job as a Technical Artist at SIS-ASTROS, where I contributed to developing the largest 3D military simulator for the Brazilian Army. Leveraging my programming background, I quickly transitioned into a Game Developer and participated in the design and development of many key features for the simulator and also for an engine used to visualize large-scale landscapes.

With six years of experience, I have expertise in scripting, graphics, optimizations, and 3D modeling. With a solid understanding of the entire artistic pipeline, I often guide artists in optimizing assets for games, or even assume both artist and programming responsibilities to develop assignments.

As a programmer and an art enthusiast, I am fascinated by combining my programming and artistic skills, including procedural generation and automated solutions for decorating scenarios. Additionally, I am passionate about tasks that present performance challenges and investigating and fixing performance bottlenecks.
